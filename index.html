Monitoring & Alerts

Know what's happening in the forest — before damage spreads.

Forest ecosystems can change quickly. Our hybrid monitoring system combines satellites, sensors, drones, and on-site AI devices to detect early signals.

  • Fire Detection Thermal and vegetation stress analysis detects hotspots early.
  • Chainsaw & Logging Alerts Acoustic AI listens for chainsaw activity — 24/7 — and notifies rangers immediately.
  • Charcoal Burning Detection Identify heat patterns linked to illegal charcoal production.
  • Biodiversity Sensors Track bee colonies, bird calls, pollinators, and ecosystem recovery.
  • Deforestation Risk Maps GIS-driven insights show where land is most vulnerable.
  • Drone-Powered Forest Intelligence High-resolution drone imagery validates growth, checks canopy gaps, and guides planting strategies.

Field Technology

Built for forests. Powered by the sun. Printed anywhere.

Forest Restored A.I includes a 3D-printable, solar-powered AI tool designed for rapid deployment inside forests and surrounding ecosystems.

It:

  • works entirely off-grid
  • collects environmental + acoustic data
  • detects logging threats and fires
  • connects to dashboards via mesh networks
  • is affordable to replace and scale
  • can be locally printed and assembled

This means communities can build, install, and maintain their own AI network — empowering local stewardship and reducing costs.

Doris Damaris

Founder & CEO, Forest Restored A.I

Doris Damaris Njagi is a visionary entrepreneur driving the intersection of climate action, sustainable design, and inclusive economic development in Africa. With a strong foundation in climate finance and banking, Doris has spent years shaping financial strategies that support green innovation and environmentally resilient businesses. Her experience in the financial sector — including structuring climate‑aligned lending products and integrating ESG frameworks within commercial banking — gives her a unique ability to link capital, impact, and community growth.

Doris founded Forest Restored A.I to tackle urgent environmental challenges such as mangrove deforestation while creating dignified livelihoods for women across Kenya's coastal, urban, and rural communities. Under her leadership, she has empowered thousands of women to generate sustainable income by transforming deforestation to thriving forest land and advancing large‑scale forest restoration with data‑driven monitoring tools.

A forward‑thinking leader, Doris is supported by a dedicated team of technologists and AI specialists, enabling Forest Restored A.I to pioneer the use of AI and IoT for ecosystem monitoring, supply‑chain efficiency, and climate‑resilient solutions. Together, they are building Africa's next generation of climate‑positive enterprises — combining local creativity with cutting‑edge technology to protect nature and uplift communities.
